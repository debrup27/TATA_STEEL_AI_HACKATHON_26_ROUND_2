## How to Read This Document

Each equipment entry contains:
- **Task** — what the machine does in one sentence
- **Inputs** — what physical quantities or materials enter the machine
- **Outputs** — what physical quantities or materials leave the machine
- **Sensors** — what is measured, at what range, at what frequency
- **Normal Operating Envelope** — the numerical bounds of healthy operation
- **Primary Failure Mode** — the dominant physical degradation mechanism (with equation)
- **Synthetic Reproduction** — exact signal curves, thresholds, and cascade triggers for simulation
